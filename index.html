A Leading Diversified Portfolio

We diversified property portfolio is the product of nearly three decades of acquisitions, adaptability and development. We own, manage, and develop quality assets in Canada’s six major markets located in high-density, transit-oriented areas.

With a strong retail core, the majority of our properties are anchored by resilient, necessity-based tenants, such as grocery, pharmacy, liquor, personal services, and specialty and value retailers. Increasingly, we also boast well-located office and residential spaces, and vibrant mixed-use communities that cater to consumer needs.
